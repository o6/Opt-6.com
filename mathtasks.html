MathTasks

Opt-6 Products is proud to introduce MathTasks, a revolutionary new comprehensive iPhone application that provides simple and quick access to the most commonly used formulas for Algebra and Geometry. Not only does it show you the formula, but it also provides built-in solvers for those formulas, making it the optimal math application for anyone to have. Best of all, it's free!

The following calculations can be performed by MathTasks:

Coordinate Geometry

-Quadratic Formula
-Slope Intercept Form
-Midpoint
-Midpoint 3D
-Distance
-Distance 3D
